Composers Contest
The International Conductors Union is honoured to announce the ICU International Composers Contest. We believe that building a creative network and bonds between composers and conductors will contribute to the development of classical music. Our goals are to find the new talents, and give them a hand in the realisation of their career, bring their beautiful music to the world.

The music of the Contest winners will be performed by the professional orchestra on the big stage lifeand published. Also, ICU Council might decide to use this music as an obligatory piece for ICU Conducting Competitions.
